# Governance

## Roles

- **Maintainer**: Full commit access, reviews and merges PRs, manages releases.
- **Contributor**: Submits PRs, reports issues, participates in discussions.

## Decision Making

Decisions are made by maintainer consensus. For significant changes, open a GitHub Discussion or Issue first.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Consistent, high-quality contributions over time. Nominated by an existing maintainer.
